Workflow

End-to-End Task Flow

⚙
Automated — Salesforce Flow

Records Created

An Underperformance Period object and a linked child object, Intervention Record, are auto-created in Salesforce under the client's deployment. The Intervention Record surfaces immediately in Client Pulse, ready for the EM to populate.

1
TaskRay Task — Per Client

Underperformance Alert

"[Client Name] — Underperformance Alert"

EM action in Client Pulse:

  1. Open Client Pulse — flagged clients appear in red
  2. Click into the client
  3. Use the performance widget to review underperformance context, read AI recommendations, forecast impact
  4. Scroll to Intervention History and log an intervention plan
  5. Fields logged in the tool sync automatically to Salesforce
2
TaskRay Task — 14 Days Later

Approval / In Market Reminder

"[Client Name] — Check In: Client Approval / In Market Status"

Flexible nudge — EM can extend the due date as needed. Open Client Pulse to update client approval and update in-market status as needed.

3
TaskRay Task — 60 Days Post In Market

60-Day Intervention Review

"[Client Name] — 60-Day Intervention Review"

Updated outcome data (% to goal, lift achieved, revenue impact) is auto-populated by Mode and pushed to Client Pulse. EM reviews and closes the intervention as Resolved or Not Resolved.

↓

Resolution

✓ Resolved

Intervention closed as Resolved. Underperformance Period closes in Salesforce. Client re-evaluated at the next detection window.

↺ Not Resolved

New Intervention Record auto-created in Salesforce and surfaced in Client Pulse. New cycle task fires — EM logs next intervention in the tool.

Data Model

Salesforce Objects

Underperformance Period

Parent record · Salesforce only · not surfaced in Client Pulse
  • Trigger Date auto-populated
  • % to Goal at Trigger auto-populated
  • Annual Enrollment Goal auto-populated
  • Campaign Mktg Touchpoints Deployed auto-populated
  • Date of Last Deployed Touchpoint auto-populated
  • Prior Underperformance Periods rollup count
  • Status Open / Resolved
  • Total Interventions rollup
  • Total Revenue Impact rollup
  • Best Lift Achieved rollup
  • Resolution Date editable
  • % to Goal at Resolution editable

Intervention Record

Child record · surfaces in Client Pulse
  • Intervention Type EM via Client Pulse
  • Status EM via Client Pulse
  • Date in Market EM via Client Pulse
  • Notes EM via Client Pulse
  • Identification Date auto-populated
  • Expected In Market Date editable
  • Confirmed In Market Date auto / override
  • % to Goal at 60 Days Mode → Client Pulse
  • Lift Achieved formula → Client Pulse
  • Revenue Impact Mode → Client Pulse
  • Outcome Resolved / Not Resolved
  • Link to Prior Intervention auto-populated
